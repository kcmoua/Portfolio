My Kitchen (Pork Belly Laab Recipe)

Wiki Page on Laab

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs pork belly
  • 1 tbsp ground rice powder
  • 1 pack Laab-Namtok Seasoning Mix
  • 2 bunches cilantro
  • 2 limes
  • 3 tbsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cut the pork belly into 6-inch strips and boil in a pot of salted water for 20 minutes.
  2. While the pork is cooking, rinse the cilantro and chop it into 1-inch cuts.
  3. Cut the cooked pork belly into thin slices.
  4. Mix the pork belly slices with a full pack of Laab-Namtok Seasoning Mix.
  5. Squeeze limes into the mix.
  6. Add the cilantro.
  7. Mix well and season to taste.
